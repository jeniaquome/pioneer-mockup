**Top 100 Questions Newcomers Have (for Skillbuilder.io):** 

## Pre-arrival:

* What neighborhood should I live in?  
  * How walkable is the neighborhood?  
  * What is street parking like?  
  * Does it fit my lifestyle?  
  * Does it fit my budget?  
  * What is the makeup of my neighborhood (e.g. families, lots of young professionals, etc.)  
  * What are the quietest neighborhoods vs. most active?  
  * How safe is my neighborhood?   
* What is the culture of the city like?  
  * How are the people?  
* What is the cost of living like?  
* Are there any up-and-coming areas to check out?  
* What is the weather / climate like?

## Housing

* How do I find reliable housing or roommates?  
* What neighborhoods are ideal for families?   
* What’s the average rent for a 1BR/2BR apartment in different neighborhoods?  
* Are utilities (water, gas, electric) included in rent? How do I set them up?


## Family and Children:

* Where should I send my child to school?  
* What K-12 programs exist?  
* What does afterschool programming look like in Pittsburgh?  
* Where/through whom can I secure childcare?  
* What parks / playgrounds are available for families?  
* What recreation and entertainment is there for children of all ages?  
* Which restaurants are family-friendly?

## Basic Needs: 

* Where is the nearest hospital?  
* What is the best grocery store near me for affordable groceries / healthy food?  
* Where do I find out about trash and recycling pickup schedules?  
* Who do I contact if I have a problem with city services (potholes, missed pickup, etc.)?  
* How do I register to vote or change my voter registration?  
* Where do I get a Pennsylvania driver's license or register my vehicle?  
* Are there immigration services organizations here?  
  * Are there cultural resources or community orgs that can support my specific needs as an international newcomer?  
* Are there local financial literacy resources or workshops?  
* How do local taxes work (e.g., city wage tax, property tax)?  
* Are there financial assistance programs for new residents, students, or low-income individuals?

## Healthcare / Wellnesss:

* How do I find a primary care doctor, dentist, or therapist?  
* What hospitals or health systems are nearby and highly rated?  
* Does Pittsburgh have community health centers or clinics with sliding scale fees?  
* How do I transfer prescriptions to a local pharmacy?  
* Are there LGBTQ+ affirming healthcare providers in the city?

## Transportation and Getting Around: 

* Will I need a car to live here comfortably?  
* I drive an EV. Where are there chargers around town? How can I find them?  
* How is the public transportation?  
  * Where can it take me?  
  * Is it reliable?  
  * Are there any transit apps that locals recommend?  
* Is biking a realistic option?  
  * Are there bike lanes or trails for commuting by bike?  
  * How do I utilize the POGOH Ebikes?  
* How far away is the airport? What’s the best way to get to/from the airport?

## Connection & Community:

* Where do people go to meet other people?  
* How do I find people with similar interests?  
* Are there community groups or meetups that I can join?  
  * How often do they happen?  
  * Where do they happen?  
  * Do they happen year-round?  
* What third places \- places to spend time outside of home and work \- are there in my neighborhood?   
* What is the best way to get involved with volunteering or civic life?  
* What faith groups exists / how can I find a spiritual practice for me?  
* What are some casual, friendly spaces to strike up a conversation (e.g., coffee shops, libraries, dog parks)?  
* Is there a place where international perspectives can thrive?

## Education

* What are the best public school districts in the area?  
* How do I register my child for school?  
* What are the best daycare or preschool options?  
* What are the best colleges in the area?   
* Are there good after-school programs?  
* What is the homeschooling or alternative education scene like?  
* What are the options for paying for college, helpful steps?

## Work and Professional Life:

* What kinds of professional opportunities are there?  
* Are there local job boards or job fairs where I can explore opportunities?   
* Are there networking opportunities for young professionals?   
* Are there mentorship opportunities?  
* Are there entrepreneurial opportunities/funders in Pittsburgh?  
* Is this an easy place to start a business? How do I go about that?   
* In what sector(s) are there opportunities? What industries are most active?  
* Are there coworking spaces where I can meet other people?  
* How do people generally commute to work?  
* Are there resources for adult learners or recent grads trying to network? 

## Coffee | F\&B 

* What are some must-try restaurants in Pittsburgh?   
* Which coffee shops can I work from?   
* Is a variety of cultural cuisines available? Where can I find ‘x’ food?

## Daily Living and Shopping:

* Where do people buy their groceries?  
  * Are there grocery stores that offer \[ethnicity / culture\] food?  
  * Are there any local farmers markets or co-ops?  
* Where can I buy furniture or households items?  
* Are there local thrift stores of “buy nothing” groups?  
* Are there local delivery services for food or groceries?

## Culture / Entertainment / Things to Do

* Where can I find entertainment?   
* Which places are open late?   
* What public libraries or learning centers are near me?  
* Where is there live music, art shows, or theater?   
* Are there any festivals to attend throughout the year?  
* What are the major cultural institutions I should know about?  
* Where can I take affordable classes (art, language, fitness)?  
* What is nightlife like, where are the best spots?

## Outdoor Recreation & Wellness

* Are there any outdoor activities or recreational opportunities around Pittsburgh?  
* What are some good day trips I can take?  
* Are there gyms, wellness centers, yoga studios, or other health amenities nearby?  
* What are some good trails in the area?

## Getting Settled In: 

* What clubs can I join?   
* Are there affinity groups that would help fulfill me?   
* Is there a neighborhood group I can join or follow along with?  
  * Do they have a newsletter?  
  * Do they host events?

## Local Knowledge & Unwritten Rules:

* What is something that locals know that I won’t?  
* Are there quirks to driving, parking, or other etiquette?  
* Where can I find a calendar of local events, festivals, or shows?  
* What are some yearly events?  
* What social media groups or Reddit threads are best for asking local questions?  
* Are there apps or resources that locals swear by?  
* What’s something people say they wish they knew before they move here?  
* What makes Pittsburgh unique from other cities?  
* What are the “must-know” Pittsburgh traditions or foods?  
* What are the sports like around the city? Seasons, sports, fan intensity etc.
